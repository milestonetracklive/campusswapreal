How It Works

We make move-out effortless and profitable.

1. Collection

During college move-out, we collect your items directly from you. No heavy lifting required.

2. Processing

We clean, sort, and prepare your items for sale, ensuring they find a second life.

3. Retail

We sell the inventory during the summer and back-to-school season.

4. Payout

Original owners receive 40% of the sale price. Make money while you sleep.

Created By Students, For Students

Every year, students dump over 100 million pounds of usable items because they lack storage options [1]. We saw this waste firsthand and decided to fix it.

Founded by Henry Russell (Davidson College) and Ben Buxbaum (Bucknell University), Campus Swap was born from the frustration of chaotic move-outs. We combine engineering efficiency with business strategy to turn "trash" back into treasure.

Campus Swap began in a small storage unit!
